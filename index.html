Poloandbike website and
online store redesign experiment

This is not a complete redesign, some elements like links to other pages are not added in this iteration.

Motivation

Poloandbike produces high quality and beautifully designed bikes. I think the current website doesn't show enough the value of the product.

Improvements

  • Bring the product to the foreground wirh big photography.
  • Improve the product page (the bike presentation) by highlighting the bike image and a highlighted sentence that quickly describes the bike.
  • Make it easier to explore sizes and colors and add the bike to the shopping cart.
